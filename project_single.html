Beautiful Liar

This is a stage drama that premiered at CCX restaurant, in Victoria Island at the heart of Lagos.

The stage drama was held twice daily for 4 consecutive days and it featured celebrities such as Erica Nlewedim, Baaj Adebule and several others. Our team created the promotional videos and pictures needed to run a digital marketing campaign for the drama. This in turn drove traffic to the venue all through the period the drama was held.
